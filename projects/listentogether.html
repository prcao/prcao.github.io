About
Listen Together is a web application that solves the hassle of requesting music at a get together. Users simply enter a YouTube video url, and the video is automatically queued up to play on the host device. Users can also upvote or downvote a song to influence the order of the playlist.

Listen Together is the first web application I have made, introduced me to many web technologies such as NodeJS, Express, React, MongoDB, and Socket.IO.
